## Introduction

Pausely ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services.

**Our Promise:** You are our customer, not our product. We never sell your personal data to third parties. We collect only what we need to provide our services, and we protect it with industry-leading security measures.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
- Profile information (optional display name)

**Subscription Data:**
- Subscription names and details you enter
- Billing amounts and frequencies
- Usage data you provide (for Smart Pause feature)
- Categories and tags you assign

**Referral Information:**
- Your unique referral code
- Referral activity (sign-ups using your code)
- Rewards earned through our referral program

**Support Communications:**
- Emails and messages sent to our support team
- Feedback and feature requests you submit

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- App feature usage and interaction patterns
- Session duration and frequency
- Crash logs and error reports (for improving stability)

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version installed
- Unique device identifiers (for authentication and security)

**Screen Time Data (Optional):**
- With your explicit permission only, we access Screen Time data to power the Smart Pause feature
- This data is processed locally on your device
- We do not transmit detailed app usage data to our servers

### 3. Information from Third Parties

**Payment Processors:**
- We use LemonSqueezy for payment processing
- We receive confirmation of subscription status (active/cancelled)
- We do NOT store your credit card information
- Full payment details are handled securely by LemonSqueezy

**Authentication:**
- We use Supabase for authentication services
- Login sessions and security tokens are managed through Supabase

We use your information for the following purposes:

### Core Service Delivery
- **Authentication:** Verifying your identity and maintaining secure sessions
- **Subscription Management:** Storing and organizing your subscription data
- **Smart Pause:** Calculating cost-per-hour and providing pause suggestions

- **Transactional Emails:** Account confirmations, password resets, billing notifications

### Improvement and Analytics
- **App Performance:** Monitoring crashes and errors to improve stability
- **Feature Usage:** Understanding which features are used most to guide development
- **Aggregated Analytics:** Anonymous, statistical analysis of user behavior

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data Is Stored

We use **Supabase** as our primary database and authentication provider:

- **Location:** Supabase databases are hosted in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Backups:** Regular automated backups with point-in-time recovery capability
- **Retention:** Your data is retained as long as your account is active

### Security Measures

We implement multiple layers of security:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Row-level security (RLS) policies ensuring you only access your own data
- Secure API endpoints with authentication required
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

**Access Controls:**
- Strict internal access controls (principle of least privilege)
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
- Audit logging of all data access

**Data Minimization:**
- We collect only what is necessary
- Screen Time data is processed locally when possible
- Usage analytics are anonymized

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Download a copy of all your data
- **Correct:** Update or modify your information

- Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account to remove all data

**Via Email:**

### Marketing Preferences

You can opt out of marketing communications:
- Tap "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email
- Go to Profile → Settings → Notifications in the app

## GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

### Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide the services you've requested
- **Legitimate Interests:** For app improvement, security, and fraud prevention
- **Consent:** For marketing communications and optional features (which you can withdraw)

### Your GDPR Rights

1. **Right to be Informed** — This Privacy Policy fulfills that right
2. **Right of Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
3. **Right to Rectification** — Correct inaccurate information
4. **Right to Erasure** — Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
5. **Right to Restrict Processing** — Limit how we use your data
6. **Right to Data Portability** — Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format
7. **Right to Object** — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
8. **Rights Related to Automated Decision Making** — We don't make automated decisions with legal effects

### Data Protection Officer

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer:
- Email: dpo@pausely.app
- Response time: Within 72 hours

### Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have violated GDPR.
