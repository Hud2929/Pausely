## Step-by-Step: Find Variant IDs

### Step 1: Go to Products
1. Open https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/products
2. Or click "Products" in the left sidebar

### Step 2: Click Your Product
You should see your products listed. Click on:
- "Pausely Pro Monthly" (or whatever you named it)

### Step 3: Look at the URL
When you're on the product page, look at the browser URL:
```
https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/products/[PRODUCT_ID]/variants/[VARIANT_ID]
```

The **VARIANT_ID** is what I need!

### Step 4: Alternative - Variant Settings
If URL doesn't show it:
1. On product page, scroll down to "Variants" section
2. Click the variant name (e.g., "Default variant")
3. Click "Settings" tab
4. Look for "Variant ID" - copy that long string

### Step 5: Get Both IDs
Do this for BOTH products:
1. **Monthly product** → Copy Variant ID
2. **Annual product** → Copy Variant ID
