## Description

### Opening Hook
Stop letting subscriptions drain your wallet. Pausely puts every recurring charge in one beautiful dashboard so you know exactly where your money goes.

### Feature Bullets
- **All subscriptions, one view** - Track Netflix, Spotify, gym memberships, and every recurring bill in a single dashboard with real-time spending totals
- **Cost-per-hour insights** - Discover what each subscription actually costs per use. That $50/mo gym membership you visit twice? Pausely shows you the real number.
- **Smart renewal alerts** - Get notified before renewals hit, detect price hikes instantly, and never be surprised by a charge again
- **One-tap cancel & pause** - Skip the support chat maze. Pausely generates direct cancel and pause links for hundreds of services
- **Find cheaper alternatives** - Our AI suggests better-value substitutes for your subscriptions, showing exactly how much you could save

### Social Proof
Join thousands of users who have identified an average of $240/year in subscription waste. Pausely users typically find 3+ forgotten subscriptions in their first week.

### Premium Features
Upgrade to Pausely Pro for unlimited subscriptions, Smart Pause suggestions, AI-powered alternative finder, advanced analytics, price change alerts, and priority support. Start with a 7-day free trial.
