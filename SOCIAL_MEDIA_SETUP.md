## 6. LINK-IN-BIO TOOL RECOMMENDATION

### Recommended: Linktree (Free Tier)

**Why:**
- Free forever tier with unlimited links
- Built-in analytics (clicks, views)
- Customizable themes to match Pausely branding
- App Store badge integration
- QR code generation for offline marketing

**Alternative: Beacons (Free Tier)**
- More design customization
- Built-in monetization features
- Email capture integration

**Link Setup:**
1. Download on the App Store (primary CTA)
2. Referral Program ("Get Pro FREE")
3. Support / Contact
4. Privacy Policy
5. Press Kit

**Pro tip:** Use UTM parameters on all links to track which platform drives the most downloads.

Example: `https://apps.apple.com/app/idYOURID?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio&utm_campaign=social`

## 7. POSTING SCHEDULE & BEST PRACTICES

### Optimal Posting Times (US Audience)

| Platform | Best Days | Best Times (EST) |
|----------|-----------|------------------|
| Instagram | Tue, Wed, Thu | 11 AM - 1 PM, 7 PM - 9 PM |
| TikTok | Tue, Thu, Fri | 7 AM - 9 AM, 7 PM - 11 PM |

### Engagement Rules

- Reply to every comment within the first hour
- Post Stories daily (Instagram)
- Use polls and question stickers in Stories
- Cross-promote TikTok videos to Instagram Reels
- Engage with finance/personal finance accounts daily (comment, like)
- Repost user-generated content with permission

### Content Production Tips

- Film all TikToks in vertical 9:16 format
- Use trending audio on TikTok (check Creative Center weekly)
- Keep Reels under 30 seconds for better completion rates
- Add captions to all videos (80% watch without sound)
- Use Pausely brand colors (luxury gold, purple, pink) in all graphics
- Maintain consistent visual style across all posts
